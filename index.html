An investigation in what makes mixed realities immersive for the users.

Francesco Vella

What is it about?

Immersion can be achieved in many different activities, reading a book or watching a movie. In fact, immersion can be generally defined as someone feeling lost in doing something. Different activities require different methodologies.

This project focuses on immersion in digital games and virtual experiences. With the help of different mechanics, realism in action, social realism and a stylised look, it tries to get the user engaged and finally immersed in the virtual space.

Game Requirements

- Unfortunatley this prototype doesn't work on MacOs.

- It requires VR goggles (the prototype was developed on oculus quest) and an oculus link cable.

- It also requires a computer that is equipped with a VR-ready graphics card.

- Other than that, enjoy the prototype and kindlygive any feedback here.
